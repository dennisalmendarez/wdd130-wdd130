Content

Home page

Welcome to the YSA Buena Vista Ward page! Here, you'll discover information about upcoming and past events. We wholeheartedly invite you to participate in these events and encourage you to bring your friends along. While most of our events take place in the church building, we occasionally organize gatherings at other locations!

We extend a warm invitation to attend our Sunday Sacrament meetings. Each meeting features two speakers and dedicated leaders who conduct the proceedings, followed by an engaging one-hour session of Sunday School where we delve into the scriptures. We believe that this experience will help you forge a deeper connection with our Savior, Jesus Christ, and our eternal Father.

Join us in this journey of spiritual growth and communal engagement. We look forward to having you with us!

Events

Welcome to our Events page! Here, you can explore the details of our upcoming events and take a trip down memory lane with highlights from past gatherings. Each event is listed with its respective date, and you'll also find exciting snapshots from our previous get-togethers.

Remember to stay updated with the latest event information and don't forget to bring your friends along! We believe that the more people join us, the richer our experiences become. Let's create lasting memories and build a vibrant community together.

About us

Our mission is to connect Young Single Adults with our church ward and extend a warm invitation to young single adults in family wards, encouraging them to join us and build meaningful connections within their age group. Through fostering these connections, we aim to strengthen their faith and spiritual growth, all while enjoying the diverse range of activities we offer.

Our vision extends beyond our immediate community. We strive to open our doors to those who may not be affiliated with our church, offering them an opportunity to experience our church not only as a place of worship but as a sanctuary of friendship and a true home of God.

Join us as we create a welcoming space for all, where friendships are formed, faith is nurtured, and a sense of belonging is fostered. Together, let's build a community that embodies the spirit of togetherness and spiritual growth.
