Upcoming and Past Events

Welcome to our Events page! We are delighted to share with you the diverse array of activities that we have in store for our vibrant community. From lively hoedowns and thrilling rodeos to energetic dances and heartwarming dinner activities, there's something for everyone to enjoy and cherish. Our events are meticulously planned to offer a blend of fun, fellowship, and spiritual nourishment. We believe that fostering a sense of togetherness and shared experiences is vital for building a strong and cohesive community. Therefore, we wholeheartedly encourage you to join us in these exciting gatherings and make the most of each moment spent together. In addition to these engaging events, we also host a weekly Family Home Evening every Monday at 7 pm. This cherished gathering provides a dedicated space for us to hear spiritual thoughts, engage in meaningful discussions, and participate in various uplifting activities. It serves as a nurturing environment where we can bond, learn, and grow together, fostering a deep sense of unity and belonging within our community. Furthermore, we are thrilled to offer an enriching institute class every Thursday at 7 pm. This class provides a unique opportunity for spiritual growth and personal development, allowing us to delve deeper into the profound teachings of our faith. Through these insightful discussions and shared learning experiences, we aim to strengthen our individual relationships with God and each other, fostering a deeper sense of purpose and understanding. Remember to stay updated with the latest event information, as we are constantly adding new and exciting activities to our calendar. Don't hesitate to invite your friends and loved ones to join us in these memorable experiences. Together, let's create lasting memories and build a vibrant and closely-knit community that fosters growth, understanding, and love. We look forward to having you join us in the upcoming events and gatherings.
